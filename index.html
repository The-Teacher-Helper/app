The Teacher Helper

Built by Alexander and Dad

We built The Teacher Helper to help make our classroom quieter.

Our Hypothesis If students hear a buzz when they get too loud, will they lower their voices?

The Teacher Helper listens to the noise level in the room. It does not record anyone. It only checks how loud the room is.

If we get too loud, it makes a buzz sound. The buzz reminds us to use quiet voices (well, we'll find out).

We built The Teacher Helper to see if the buzz would help students talk quieter and keep our class calm.

🤖

We're not experts in coding, but we used AI to help us code this app to test our hypothesis. Along the way, we also learned a lot about how AI can help us build things.
